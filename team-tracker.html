Why this matters

A single team's footprint is small.
Forty-eight teams' isn't.

FIFA reports an average team travel distance of 5,146 miles (8,281 km) for 2026 — up from Russia 2018 (5,441 mi) but well below Brazil 2014 (7,054 mi). Across 48 teams, that's roughly 400,000 km of team-only movement, before any fan travel is counted.

What varies most

Not the distance. The mode.

A charter flight from Kansas City to Los Angeles emits ~7× more CO₂e per passenger than the same distance by coach bus. Teams based near their fixture cluster (Brazil in New Jersey, Belgium in Seattle) get to bus most of their games; teams criss-crossing between coasts don't have that option.
